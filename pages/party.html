Planning a Party?

Planet Pizza is the perfect location for your hosting a fun party that's out of this world! Our restaurants are fully equipped for your next birthday party event with different party packages to suit your needs. When planning a party with us, you'll have access to all the party food you could want, one of our private party rooms, and the astronomical arcade! Our arcade is fully equipped with games, prizes, and the claw... machine.

When planning a party, please call to reserve at least a week in advance. This lets us ensure that your party experience will be super! A typical party will be allowed to run for up to two hours, but extensions can be made for an additonal payment. *Please note that hours of availability for parties may vary by restaurant.

So what are you waiting for? Plan your party with one of our packages below!
